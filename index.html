For fun.

Create your own mini-league with your mates and see who has the best football knowledge.

I'll display the leaders in each mini-league in the reseults.

Do I have to pay to enter?

No, it is free. Hence why there is no prize.

However, if you and your mates, team, or workmates etc, want to put some money on it, then use the mini-league feature. Make sure everyone knows the name of the league, and get them to add it on the entry form. I'll keep track of who is leading.

You'll have to deal with the money part yourself.

How do you score points?

Check this points chart. The better you predict the results, the more points you get.

Generally, based on past competitions, if you pick the actual winner of the world cup, and one of the other semi-finalists, you probably will win.

But what if I end up with the same points at the end?

Yeah, this happened once previously, so I've now have added a bonus tie-break question.

Pick the score in the final, and whoever is closest will win the tie-break.
