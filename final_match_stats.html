Fair Play Award:
===============================================================

I didn't think about the ability to pick the fair play award to the competition 
until after it started. I'll add it to the next competition.

Based on the following rules by Fifa teams scores points,
  1 yellow = -1pt,
  indirect red = -3pts, (i.e. 2 yellows - only happenend once so far)
  direct red = -4tps,
  yellow and direct red = -5pts.
Least points assigned is the cleanest team.

The Czech Republic and Tunisia had only one yellow card from their 3 games, 
giving them -1 points total, at an average 0.33 points per game.

New Zealand was ranked 9th with 4 yellows from 3 games, for -4 points 
at an average of -1.33 point per game

Of the four teams left:

Spain and France are tied for 22 place, with 6 yellow cards giving them -6 pts 
at an average of 0.9 pts per game.

Argentina is 32nd with 8 yellows, giving them -8 pts at an average of 1.1pts per game.

And the dirtiest team of the final four is England in 44th place with 8 yellows 
and 1 direct red, giving them -12 pts at an average of 1.7 pts per game.
