UC Master Yodas' World Cup Football 2026 Competition - The Cobras
The Yodas are a Masters football team playing for the University of Canterbury in New Zealand, many of whom have been playing together for 30 years.

Several of our members like donuts and also play for the Cobras - also known as the Canterbury Police Football team.

We are helping to support the Canterbury Police Football team to go to The Australasian Emergency Services games.

Can you help the COBRAs travel to Australia?

Canterbury Police Football from New Zealand is a passionate amateur team representing our district.

After winning the Police National Football Championship, we’re preparing to compete at the Australasian Police & Emergency Services Games in Queensland, October 2026.

We’re fundraising to cover essential costs such as travel, accommodation, and registration so our players can represent Canterbury on the international stage.

For as little as $2 you can help them, by donating when you enter the Yodas' World Cup Football competition.
